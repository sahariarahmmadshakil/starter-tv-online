A day-in-the-life of four women entrepreneurs. See their process. Get insight on the motivations behind their ambition.

Episode 1

DRIVEN by Survival

Latricia Wilder left a $300K job in media sales and emptied her retirement account in order to open a fitness studio - Vibe Ride Detroit. Circumstances beyond her control are threatening Vibe Ride Detroit’s survival. However, Latricia is determined to not go down without a fight.

Episode 2

DRIVEN by Mission

South Central Los Angeles is being revitalized. Realtor Sunny Jones grew up in South Central during the tumultuous 1980’s. Sunny is on a mission to ensure that her community gets their piece of South Central’s new landscape via residential and commercial property ownership.

Episode 3

DRIVEN by Confidence

Breann Cook is the founder of the streetwear fashion brand, Ambition is the New Pink. She credits her success to her education, industry experience and her family. Now, Breann wants to pay it forward by sharing her knowledge in high schools across America.

Episode 4

DRIVEN by Legacy

Lauren Stovall is a 2nd-generation black co-owner of Hot Sam’s – the oldest menswear clothing store in downtown Detroit. Hot Sam’s was founded by a Jewish family in 1921. Lauren’s father and his business partner purchased Hot Sam’s in 1994 after working at the store for 20 years.

A message from our founder:

“Black people on TV!”

Growing up, any appearance of a black person on television was a symbol of excellence and a very big deal. However, today's representation, especially of black women, has regressed into harmful stereotypes. With StartHER TV, my goal is to change that by showcasing black and brown women's creativity, resilience, and humanity, leaving viewers feeling empowered and hopeful. Our unique focus on black and brown women entrepreneurs, through unscripted and scripted content, sets StartHER TV apart and empowers us to take control of our narratives.
