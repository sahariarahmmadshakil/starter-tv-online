We Filmed DRIVEN at Some Amazing Black-Owned Locations

Step into the captivating world of StartHER TV's inaugural "Jumpstart" series - DRIVEN. Discover the lives of a remarkable group of women of color entrepreneurs like never before, as they generously share their exclusive day-to-day experiences.



Hot Sam’s Detroit
127 Monroe Ave, Detroit, MI
Website: https://hotsamsdetroit.com

Hot Sam's wasn't merely a filming location for Lauren Stovall's episode of DRIVEN, but rather an integral part of the story being told. Founded 102 years ago by a Jewish family, Hot Sam’s transitioned to black-owned when Cliff Green and Tony Stovall, two Hot Sam’s employees, purchased the store. Hot Sam’s continues to thrive today. It’s an amazing story that you learn more about in DRIVEN.


*The next two locations where we filmed are particularly significant for two reasons: first, they are black-owned, and second, the owners of these businesses also own the buildings where their businesses are situated.



ORA Energy (formerly, Hot & Cool Café)
4331 Degnan Blvd., Los Angeles, CA
IG: @oraenergy

Meeting with a first-time homebuyer client and talking business over coffee at the cutest café situated in Leimert Park, which is known as a hub for African-American culture in Los Angeles, was just part of Sunny Jones’ day.



Simply Wholesome
4508 WEST Slauson Avenue, Los Angeles, CA
(Corner of Slauson & Overhill)
Website: https://www.simplywholesome.com

In this scene, Breann and her mother, Tanya, order fresh organic juices and flavorful patties from this legendary Los Angeles health-focused restaurant.
